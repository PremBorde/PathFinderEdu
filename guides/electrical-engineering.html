Electrical Engineering

A comprehensive guide to building your career in Electrical Engineering

Overview

Electrical Engineering focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. This field offers diverse opportunities in power generation, electronics, control systems, and renewable energy.

Required Skills

Technical Skills

  • Circuit Design & Analysis
  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Digital Electronics
  • Programming (C++, MATLAB)

Soft Skills

  • Problem Solving
  • Analytical Thinking
  • Project Management
  • Team Collaboration
  • Technical Documentation

Education Path

Bachelor's Degree (B.Tech/B.E.)

Duration: 4 years

Eligibility: 12th with PCM, JEE Main/State CETs

Key Subjects: Circuit Theory, Power Systems, Control Systems, Electromagnetics

Master's Degree (M.Tech)

Duration: 2 years

Eligibility: B.Tech/B.E., GATE qualified

Specializations: Power Systems, Control Systems, Power Electronics

Career Opportunities

Power Systems Engineer

Design and maintain electrical power systems

Control Systems Engineer

Design automated control systems

Average Salary: ₹5-25 LPA

Electronics Engineer

Average Salary: ₹3-35 LPA
