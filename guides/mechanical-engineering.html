Mechanical Engineering

A comprehensive guide to building your career in Mechanical Engineering

Overview

Mechanical Engineering deals with the design, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It's one of the broadest engineering disciplines, offering careers in automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, and energy sectors.

Education Path

Bachelor's Degree (B.Tech/B.E.)

Duration: 4 years

Eligibility: 12th with PCM, JEE Main/State CETs

Key Subjects: Thermodynamics, Machine Design, Manufacturing, Fluid Mechanics

Master's Degree (M.Tech)

Duration: 2 years

Eligibility: B.Tech/B.E., GATE qualified

Specializations: Robotics, Automotive, Manufacturing Technology
