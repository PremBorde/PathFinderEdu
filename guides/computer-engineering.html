Computer Engineering

A comprehensive guide to building your career in Computer Engineering

Overview

Computer Engineering combines principles of computer science and electrical engineering to develop computer software and hardware systems. This dynamic field offers opportunities in software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and more.

Required Skills

Technical Skills

  • Programming (Java, Python, C++)
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Management
  • Web Development
  • Operating Systems

Soft Skills

  • Problem Solving
  • Analytical Thinking
  • Team Collaboration
  • Communication
  • Time Management

Education Path

Bachelor's Degree (B.Tech/B.E.)

Duration: 4 years

Eligibility: 12th with PCM, JEE Main/State CETs

Key Subjects: Programming, Data Structures, Computer Architecture, Operating Systems

Master's Degree (M.Tech)

Duration: 2 years

Eligibility: B.Tech/B.E., GATE qualified

Specializations: AI/ML, Data Science, Information Security

Career Opportunities
